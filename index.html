As an Executive and Life Coach, I work with a wide range of clients and offer a highly personalized approach tailored to each individual. In a supportive atmosphere, I help clients attain the professional and personal growth they're striving for. Learn more about me

I offer sessions, both in-person, on Zoom, Face Time and on the phone. We will start with a free initial assessment to clearly define your short and long term goals, everything from communication skills to personal acceptance. We will use these goals as a foundation to create a strategy for attaining these objectives.

As an Executive and Life Coach, I specialize in the following areas:

  • Leadership and Management Skills
  • Communication Skills
  • Branding and Culture
  • Strategic Thinking/Planning
  • Career Transition
  • Personality Assessment
  • Emotional and Social Intelligence Awareness
  • Organizational Development
  • Team Coaching
  • Time management and Productivity
  • Professional and Personal Growth
  • Work-Life Balance
  • Mindfulness and Ayuryoga
  • Diversity and multi-cultural Challenges
  • Private, public and non-profit sectors
